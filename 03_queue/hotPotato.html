An example of a circular queue is the Hot Potato game.
In this game, children are organized in a circle.
Before the game starts, they determine a number - num,
and they pass the hot potato to their neighbor
and the hot potato stops being passed at the num-th child.
This num-th child will be removed from this game.

This action is repeated until there is only one child left.
And, that is the winner for this game.
